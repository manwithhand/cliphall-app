cliphall

Cliphall is a way for you to record clips and share it with your friends. You're able to record in the background and not use up any space! But when you get a cool clip you want to share, all you have to do is hit a key on your keyboard that you assigned! If you decide to save it, it will be saved onto your computer, but if you don't want to save it then it wont take up any space.
